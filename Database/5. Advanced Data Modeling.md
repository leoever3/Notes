### Extended entity relationship model

EER: adding more semantic constructs to ER model

* **Supertypes**: contain common characteristics 
  * Entity supertype is a generic entity type related to one or more entity subtypes
* **Subtypes**:  contain unique characteristics of each enitity subtype
* Specialization hierarchy: "**is-a**" relationship
* **Inheritance**
  * Subtypes inherit **PK** from super types
  * Supertype and its subtypes maintain a 1:1 relationship

##### Subtype discriminator

* **Disjoint** subtypes: unique subset (graduate or undergraduate but not both)
* **Overlapping**: non unique subset
* **Partial** completeness
* **Total** completeness

##### Specialization and Generalization

* Specialization: **top-down**
* Generalization: **bottom-up**

##### Clusters

* Entity cluster is **virtual** entity type



#### Primary key

* Guarantee entity **integrity**
* PK + FK implement **relationships**

* Natural key or **Surrogate key**
* Composite PK
  * weak entities
